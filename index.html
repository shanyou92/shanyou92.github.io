Shan You 游山

Make it simple. Make it work.

I am currently an Associate Research Director at SenseTime Research working with Fei Wang and Chen Qian. Before that, I finished a two-year post doc at Tsinghua University supervised by Prof. Changshui Zhang (IEEE Fellow). I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science from the School of EECS, Peking University in 2019, advised by Prof. Chao Xu. I am also very hornored to be jointly supervised by Prof. Dacheng Tao (IEEE Fellow) when visiting the UBTech Sydney AI Institute, the University of Sydney. In 2014, I received my bachelor's degree both in mathematics and applied mathematics (elite class) and in English at Xi'an Jiaotong University.

  youshan@sensetime.com                               Google Scholar

Research Interest

My research interests include fundamental algorithms for machine learning and computer vision, such as efficient machine learning, representation learning, automl and face analysis.
